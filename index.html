Protractor

Protractor is a convenient angle meter, simple and easy to use. The application allows you to measure angles in pictures, photos or from the camera in real time. The ability to measure an unlimited number of angles simultaneously. Scaling of the measured object allows more accurate determination of angles. High accuracy of measurement, as well as the ability to change the colors of instruments, allows you to measure angles easily, quickly and very accurately.
